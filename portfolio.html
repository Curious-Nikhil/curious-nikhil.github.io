TEJAS - Model Rocket

This took over a year for the development and testing phases. Building a model rocket from scratch requires, building up the infrastructure first. This consists of Thrust Bench, Launchpad services and Rocket Motor Dev. The on board flight computer is built with fault tolerant design philosophy. It performs data logging of 10 channel stream at 30 times per second. More features: Killswitch, Apogee Detection, Safety Net.
